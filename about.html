About OfMaT
How it started?
It was created by Rui Figueira during Masters Course held at Salvador University. The idea came when he observed the lack of network management tools involving openflow and virtualized networks. OfMaT was developed using the FlowVisor API and Floodlight API. The main advantage of this tool is the integrated monitoring and management of openflow devices and network slices.
